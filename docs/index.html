Why Vexra

Wireless Trackpad

High-precision multi-touch gestures. Tap, scroll, pinch, and swipe — just like a real trackpad.

Full Keyboard

Complete text input with modifiers, shortcuts, function keys, and media controls. Zero latency.

PIN Secured

6-digit PIN authentication on every session. Single device limit. No cloud, no data leaves your network.

Precision Gestures

Every gesture you'd expect — and more.

1-finger drag Move cursor
1-finger tap Left click
1-finger hold Click & Drag
2-finger scroll Scroll up/down
2-finger tap Right click
3-finger swipe up Task View
3-finger swipe down Show Desktop
3-finger swipe L/R Switch apps
4-finger swipe up/down Volume control
4-finger swipe L/R Virtual desktops

How it Works

Up and running in 60 seconds

1

Install

Download the APK on your phone and run Vexra.exe on your PC. That's it — no setup wizards.

2

Connect

Enter the IP address and optional PIN shown on your desktop receiver. Tap Connect.

3

Use

Your phone is now a wireless trackpad and keyboard. Swipe, tap, type — zero latency.

Ready to go wireless?

Download the Vexra receiver for your desktop and the app for your phone.
